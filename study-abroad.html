Study Abroad Services

Comprehensive support for your international education journey.

Educating the world to make a Difference

Sahara is a professional recruiting and training company providing students and parents with current information about studies offered globally. Sahara Services offer information about the selected destination, the universities, programs, admissions, accommodations and student Visas. Sahara was established by Dr Mikey the Chairman of this company, the chairman is highly qualified from the UK and held Doctor of Philosophy (PhD), MA, BA, PG Cert in Education (MA), Fellow in Higher Education Academy UK and Certificate of Doctoral Supervision. with a longstanding reputation as one of UKs leading education agencies.

Our Head Office is in Bournemouth, UK and maintains excellent contact with the Admissions and Academic sectors of the institutions we represent. These relationships ensure our clients receive the best possible service and enable us to advocate on behalf of our clients when the need arises. Students can complete our Assessment form in order to assess their eligibility to study abroad.
