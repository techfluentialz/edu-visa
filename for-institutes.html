SERVICES & BENEFITS FOR EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS

Sahara Student Services has vast experience for recruiting International students to international institutes. Why Choose Us

We provide full service at every step.

STUDENT COUNSELLING

We have very close links with overseas Universities and Colleges. We frequently visit these institutes, giving presentations about the institute/institutes which can be of interest to the students.

OUTDOOR PROMOTION

We effectively plan and promote program of the institutes we represent. Effective methods of advertisement including print media and electronic media are used.

ADVERTISING ADVICE

We advise our client institutions on a systematic advertising schedule, campaign; Selection of media and frequency of advertisements.

FOLLOW UP

Data collected in response to advertisements, seminars, meetings etc is properly managed and we constantly follow up students by writing them letters about you and calling them on telephone.

IMAGE BUILDING

Sahara Student services gives top priority to image building of its partner institutes. We persuade our prospective students by telling them why they should choose your institute.

PRE-ASSESSMENT OF PROSPECTIVE STUDENTS

We make initial assessments at our office about the students interested in your institution by looking whether he/she meets entry requirements or not and if not then guide them what a student needs to support his application.

PRESENTATIONS

We have very close links with overseas Universities and Colleges. We frequently visit these institutes, giving presentations about the institute/institutes which can be of interest to the students.

MARKET UPDATES

We update institutions on educational issues and developments, which have a bearing on the enrolment of students.

ORGANIZING ON-SPOT ADMISSIONS

We assist you during your On-Spot admission sessions. This may include holding seminars, taking entry tests or interviews. We are always there to help you.

ARRANGING SEMINARS AND INFO SESSIONS

If you are traveling overseas, we provide complete services in arrangement of your seminars including promotion in local media and arranging suitable location.

SUPPORT DURING EDUCATION FAIRS AND SEMINARS

Our staff is always ready to support you at education fairs, exhibitions, seminars and other functions participated by your institution.

MAKING NEW PROPOSALS

We suggest new proposals to the institutions for promoting their educational products and also suggest new courses of study according to market demands.

WE SIMPLIFY AND ENSURE YOUR INTERNATIONAL GROWTH

Sahara has a wide variety of features which will simplify your international operations and increase the application conversion rate.

YOUR OFFICE ABROAD

We are your team working at your overseas office reducing your costs and burden: and producing quality results with quick response.

EXPLORING POTENTIAL FOR YOU

Exploring market potential for institutions and establishing links with universities/colleges to create a broad recruitment base.
